🏗️ ARQUITECTURA
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

KeyBindings.java (constantes)
       ↓
KeyBindingManager.java (singleton/ejecutor)
       ↓
AppLauncher (inicializa en start())
       ↓
Scene (agreg event filter global)
       ↓
Usuario presiona atajo
       ↓
handleKeyPress() evalúa teclas
       ↓
executeAction() ejecuta Runnable
       ↓
Actualiza SettingsController
       ↓
Sincroniza SoundManager/VideoPlayer
